Experience Adventure and Fun: Quindío offers an array of exciting activities for adventure seekers. Explore the National Coffee Park, an amusement park that combines thrilling rides with educational coffee experiences. Saddle up for horseback riding adventures through the picturesque landscapes or enjoy the adrenaline rush of canopy ziplining. Quindío promises endless excitement for all ages.


Coffee Park offers a unique experience where visitors can learn about the history, culture, and production process of coffee in an interactive and entertaining way. Here are some key features and activities you can expect to find at Coffee Park:


Coffee Museum: The park has a dedicated coffee museum that provides insight into the history and significance of coffee in Colombia. You can learn about the cultivation, harvesting, processing, and roasting of coffee beans.


Coffee Plantation: Coffee Park has its own working coffee plantation where visitors can see coffee plants, learn about the growth cycle, and even participate in the coffee-picking process during the harvest season.


Coffee Shows and Demonstrations: Throughout the day, Coffee Park hosts various shows and demonstrations related to coffee production. These include traditional Colombian dance performances, coffee tasting sessions, and barista demonstrations.


Amusement Rides: The park features a variety of amusement rides suitable for all ages. From roller coasters and water rides to carousels and train rides, there are plenty of attractions to enjoy.


Cultural Exhibits: Coffee Park showcases the cultural heritage of the Coffee Triangle region. You can explore traditional architecture, visit replicas of traditional Colombian towns, and enjoy exhibitions that highlight the local arts and crafts.


Adventure Activities: For those seeking adventure, Coffee Park offers thrilling activities such as zip-lining, bungee jumping, and an aerial tramway that provides panoramic views of the surrounding coffee plantations and landscapes.


Gastronomy: As expected, Coffee Park offers a range of food and beverage options, including coffee-themed treats and traditional Colombian cuisine.


Coffee Park is an educational and entertaining destination that provides visitors with an immersive experience into the world of coffee. It combines amusement park attractions, cultural exhibits, and coffee-related activities to create a memorable visit for tourists interested in learning about Colombia's coffee culture.
